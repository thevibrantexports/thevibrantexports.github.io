Castor Oil & Derivatives

It’s monopoly commodity in our Province, world’s 75% production in Gujarat India.

Also we accept contract manufacturing and production facility.

A range of pharmaceutical-grade castor oil is produced from cold pressing these castor beans. Virgin castor oil is considered to be the highest quality oil. Extracted under the norms of European Pharmacopeia, Virgin Castor Oil EP Grade is a high-refined quality castor oil that is used in the parental preparation of products. It has low moisture and less acid value in it. The castor seeds are cold-pressed followed by a refining method to obtain superior quality of organic virgin castor oil. This brilliantly clear and viscous Virgin Castor Oil EP Grade is mostly used in pharmaceutical applications as an emollient for creams and lotions to protect the skin against harsh environmental conditions.

Uses:
  • As a stiffening agent
  • As laxatives
  • For capsule-coating
  • Products used to heal skin eruptions
  • In aromatherapy products & massage oil
  • As a conditioning agent in hair care products
Types:
  • Commercial Grade Castor Oil
  • REFINED (F.S.G) / B.S.S. GRADE CASTOR OIL
  • Pharma EP Grade Virgin Castor Oil
  • BP Grade Castor Oil
  • Pale Pressed Grade (PPG) Castor Oil
  • Cold Pressed Grade Castor Oil
  • USP Grade Castor Oil
  • DEHYDRATED CASTOR OIL (DCO)
  • De Oiled Castor Cake (DOC)
