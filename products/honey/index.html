Purest Honey/Shahad | 100% Pure & Natural | Pesticide Free

Honey is one of the most sought-after ingredients in the world. It is used in various cultures and in different types of cuisines. Our raw unprocessed honey is collected from bees that we keep at the West zone, Gujarat. The bees from which it is derived from has the scientific classification “Apis cerana indica”,Apis Melifera and is found in the western region of India. Pure shahad is widely used as a sweetener or a flavoring agent in savory dishes. It typically has a fresh aroma and depending on its nectar source honey can be floral, fruity, spicy, nutty or earthy. Honey contains several antioxidants, including phenolic compounds like flavonoids which has various medicinal properties.

Our pure wild honey is collected from bees that we keep at our farm in Gujarat. We have expert beekeepers who tend to the honeybees throughout the year and carefully collect honey from the beeswax without damaging the hive or hurting the honeybees. Our honey is packed in our hygienic processing facility and retains its natural freshness, aroma and nutrients.

100% NATURAL & PESTICIDE FREE: We follow sustainable growing practices for fruits and spices at our farm, without the use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides. Our raw honey is collected from our farm, which guarantees that it does not contain any chemicals, pesticides or insecticides.

WHOLESOME & PURE: Unlike commercial brands, we give you unprocessed raw honey which is packed with nutrients and antioxidants. We don’t heat or ultrafilter our honey, which ensures that all the essential nutrients in the honey is retained.

HEALTHY & NUTRITIOUS FOOD: Authentic unprocessed shahad is probably one of the most nutritious foods in the world. It is loaded with antioxidants, which prevents cells from being damaged by free radicals. Honey naturally contains hydrogen peroxide, an antiseptic, which helps fight fungal and bacterial infections. Regular consumption of honey has been known to aid in improving metabolism and accelerate weight-loss.
